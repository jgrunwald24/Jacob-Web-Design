Extra Moist Vanilla Cake

Ingredients:

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Full fat milk
  • Sugar
  • Oil(1 tablespoon)
  • Vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Whip eggs and sugar
  2. Gradually add flour
  3. Melt butter into milk in microwave
  4. Mix batter into hot milk
  5. Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs
  6. Pour into 2 cake pans
  7. (Optional) Knock out big air bubbles
  8. Bake 30 minutes
  9. Cool upside down
  10. Decorate!
