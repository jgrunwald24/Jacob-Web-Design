Author's Note

I chose this subject because environmental health is the number one issue in the world today, and landfills, and the pollution they cause, are one of the many direct results of humanity's excess. I chose these colors because I found them pleasant to look at but still evocative of a pale sunrise like in the first photo on the main home page. I chose the font because I felt it had impact to highlight the importance of the matter. Lastly, this layout was chosen because it is imformative and easy to follow.
