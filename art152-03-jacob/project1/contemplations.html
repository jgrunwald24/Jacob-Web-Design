Seven Contemplations

Seven Contemplations is an installation of seven monumental works that craft a meditative journey for the viewer. Curry responds to the cavernous architecture of the 8,000 square foot Albright-Knox Northland with immersive works that cultivate a feeling of awe by looking outward to the immensity of the cosmos, and inward to our interior worlds.
