Haven

Curry focuses on two encounters from these collaborations: Maram, a young Syrian woman, and Daniel, a young man from Afghanistan. A two-story-tall portrait of Maram laughing is the centerpiece of the exhibition, installed in the Skissernas Museum’s courtyard, capturing the sense of hope that Maram feels as she settles into her new life. The exhibition extends into the stairwell and Birgit Rausing Gallery, responding to the gallery’s architectural elements with screen-printed pattern wallpaper overlaid with Curry’s iconic portraits, capturing the feeling of safety, comfort and home.
